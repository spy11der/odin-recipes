Korean Beef

Description

This a recipe on korean beef. a staple in many homes abd soon to be one in yours as we ll. This recipe will be the best one you ever try.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 ground beef
  • brown sugar
  • red pepper flakes
  • cinnamon stick
  • star anise
  • sesame oil
  • butter
  • msg
  • soy sauce
  • minced ginger
  • gochujang
  • veggie stock

Steps

  1. Brown one pond of ground beef in a pan on medium heat
  2. Once your ground beef is browned you’ll drain the fat
  3. Add 2 tbs of butter, 1/4 cup of soy sauce, 1/3 cup of brown sugar, 1 tbs of minced and peeled ginger, 1 tbs of msg, 1 tbs of sesame oil. Continue cooking until everything is incorporated.
  4. Add 1 cup of veggie stock, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 star anise pods, and 1 tbs of gochujang sauce. Turn down heat to low and cook for 15min
  5. Add 1 tsp of red pepper flakes and cook for 5mins more
  6. Enjoy!
