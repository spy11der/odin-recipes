Cornbread

Description

This is southern staple dish. One the main dishes on the table for dinner. And one of my favorite dishes on the table. I fell in love with the dish when I was a little kid and have loved this dish since. I make this dish often in the firehouse and they swear its cake. but I hope you enjoy it as much as I do and everyone else who eats it.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups yellow cornmeal self rising
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 2 sticks unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • honey

Equipment

  • glass baking dish
  • Or a cast iron skillet

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Melt 2 sticks of butter
  3. Measure dry ingredients: sugar, cornmeal and flour
  4. Measure out milk and crack eggs in a bowl
  5. Mix in dry ingredients to the bowl with wet ingredients slowly until fully combined
  6. Add honey and vanilla extract no measurements. I like a lot of both so if you want the same one people love add 1 TBS vanilla and 1/3 cup of honey or more!
  7. Get your pan of choice
  8. Glass dish melt 1 TBS of butter or the a stick on the bottom and sides Cast iron skillet pop it in the oven while its preheating and put a 1 TBS of bacon grease in the pan while its preheating at 425
  9. Pour batter in glass dish and bake at 350 for 25mins or longer
  10. Cast iron: once oven has preheated take out skillet and pour batter in it should only take 25mins.
  11. Remove dishes of choice from oven after the time and use a butter knife or toothpick and stick in to check doneness. if it comes out clean its ready if not cook longer in 5min increments.
  12. Enjoy and let me know how you did
